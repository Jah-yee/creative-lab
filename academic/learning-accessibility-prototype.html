Cellular respiration is the process that converts nutrients into energy in the form of ATP.

Overview
  • Definition: A metabolic process occurring in cells

  • Requires: Oxygen (for aerobic respiration)
Main Stages

  2. Citric Acid Cycle: Further breaks down pyruvate derivatives
  3. Electron Transport Chain: Generates most ATP through oxidative phosphorylation
