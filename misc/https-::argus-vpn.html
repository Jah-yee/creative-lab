Popular questions

Are VPN providers secure?

Some VPN providers may log your internet activity or have vulnerable security systems. When choosing a provider, it’s important to pay attention to privacy policies, encryption levels, and more.

How does ArgusVPN work?

ArgusVPN conceals your IP address and routes internet traffic through a secure VPN tunnel using its proprietary encryption technology, ensuring security and confidentiality while maintaining exceptional internet speed.

Furthermore, we adhere to a strict no-logs policy, ensuring your data's security and preventing access by third parties, including our own services.

Is there a free plan?

Unfortunately, no. We do have a free trial option that gives you full access to all of our premium features. You can evaluate our service and decide if it meets your needs.

How to start using ArgusVPN?

Choose the iOS or Android platform that suits you and install ArgusVPN, connect to any country with just one click or trust it to our algorithm that will find the best option automatically.

Are there any traffic limits when using ArgusVPN?

No, ArgusVPN does not impose any traffic limits. You can use an unlimited amount of data for secure and anonymous internet surfing.
