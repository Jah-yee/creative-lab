Tutorial

How to Use eyeGuide

Follow these simple steps to get the most out of eyeGuide. Select a topic below to learn more.

Signal Detection

1. Position Camera at Intersection

Hold your phone up at eye level, pointing toward traffic signals. Keep the phone steady for best detection.

2. Receive Feedback

eyeGuide will provide audio, haptic, and visual feedback about traffic signal status and when it's safe to cross.

3. Crossing Guidance

The app will help you align with the crosswalk and provide countdown timers for crossing windows.

Tips for Best Results
  • tips_and_updates
    Hold your phone steady for more accurate signal detection
  • tips_and_updates
    Use headphones for clearer audio guidance in noisy environments
  • tips_and_updates
    Position yourself directly in front of the crosswalk for best results
  • tips_and_updates
    The SOS button is always available during detection mode
